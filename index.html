Let's explore the 'Vaccine Distribution Challenges'

Goal of ‘Vax India Tech’ is to identify and address challenges of COVID-19 vaccine distribution in India. TRIZ (Theory of Inventive Problem Solving) model to reach effective solutions. Compartmental models (SIR and SEIR) to understand future projections. End to end depiction of vaccine journey. Integration of RFID, IoT temperature sensors, Blockchain and more. Culmination will be a set of policies to ensure equitable and efficient vaccine distribution in India.
